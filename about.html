About Us

ERamen shop is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. It was established since 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia since 2015. All ERamen menu made by organic flour and turn into delicious ramen noodle. Customer can find any kind of ramen, such as ramen and so many kind of tempura.

A restaurant that specializes in ramen dishes, the wheat-flour Japanese noodles in broth. In Japan, ramen shops are very common and popular, and are sometimes referred to as ramen-ya

Our Vision & Mission

To be the main and foremost goal in providing customers with an authentic and satisfying ramen eating experience.


1. Serving high-quality ramen: Providing ramen with the best quality ingredients and using authentic recipes to create an authentic and delicious taste of ramen.


2. Provides a wide variety of menus: Offers a wide variety of ramen variations, including classic broth ramen, vegetarian ramen, and ramen with a wide selection of meats and other toppings, catering to different tastes and preferences of customers.


3. Excellent customer service: Providing friendly, fast and efficient service to every customer. Ensuring customer satisfaction by maintaining quality and friendliness standards in every interaction.
